What is Flower Buckets?

Flower Buckets is a student run fundraiser created to benefit the building of Blue Note Junction!
Flower Buckets hopes to:

  • Provide much needed funds to Blue Note Junction.
  • Promote sustainable growing practices in Asheville.
  • Make growing your own flowers at home fun!

Blue Note Junction will be a BIPOC centered Health and Wealth generator that is planned to be built in West Asheville. Featuring gardens, a farmers market, saunas, and much more, Blue Note Junction will cultivate community healing and promote economic growth. Help us promote community growth by growing flowers!

How Much

Buy each Flower Bucket on a sliding scale of what you want to pay from $10 to $15. 100% of the proceeds go to Blue Note Junction.

Flowers Buckets are to be picked from the Peace Gardens in Asheville on April 9th between 9am and 3pm. (If this time doesn't work for you, please contact us to set up a separate pick-up time.)
